Shipping & Returns

Our products are carefully chosen and are passed through quality checkpoints before packaging and delivering. We guarantee the delivery of high-quality and fresh products by a well-trained staff using refrigerated vehicles. As A&H values your time, we will deliver your order on time.

  • Delivery is free for orders worth 50 AED (excl. VAT) or more.
  • Orders less than 50 AED (excl. VAT) will be charged 10 AED + 5% VAT.
    Total Delivery Charge including VAT = 10.5 AED.
  • Orders can be placed 24/7.
  • Delivery timings are 8:00 AM – 10:00 PM.
  • Delivery will be based on the convenient date & time selected from available appointments during checkout.
  • Some appointments may not be available if already booked by other clients.
  • We commit to deliver on time, but delays may occur due to weather, traffic, or technical issues.
  • The order will be confirmed by an email sent to your registered email address.
  • If items are unavailable or delivery issues arise, we will contact you by phone and send an email notification.
  1. Payment on Delivery – Cash
  2. Payment on Delivery – Bank Card
  3. Payment on Website – Bank Card
  4. Smile Program Points (from your Smile account)

Note: Customers must pay the full order amount using one payment type.
